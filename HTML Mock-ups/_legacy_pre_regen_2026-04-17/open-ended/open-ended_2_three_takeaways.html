Qualitative Feedback

Open-Ended Summary

"Are there any specific areas where you feel the leadership team could improve in order to better support employees at Surge?"

Communication & Transparency

A significant portion of the feedback centers on the need for improved communication from leadership. This includes clearer articulation of corporate strategy, long-term goals, and the rationale behind decisions. Employees desire more consistent engagement, such as "fireside chats" or casual meetings, to foster trust and alignment across the organization. There is also a call for more timely communication regarding changes in policies or supervisor absences.

Empowerment & Recognition

Several comments highlight a desire for increased employee empowerment and trust, with concerns raised about micromanagement. Employees want to feel valued and recognized for their contributions, with suggestions for giving credit to those who excel. There's also a call for more investment in employee development, cross-functional collaboration, and acknowledging the impact of each department on the organization's success.

Accountability & Fairness

Feedback indicates a need for greater accountability within teams, with leadership addressing performance issues more proactively. Ensuring policies are applied equally across the organization is also a key concern. Additionally, there are suggestions for improvements to compensation, benefits, and work-life balance, such as increased work-from-home flexibility and personal days for contractors. The on-boarding process for contractors needs improvement.
